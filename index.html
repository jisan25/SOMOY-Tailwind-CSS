Main Features

Subjectivity

Our perception of time is subjective. This means that two people may experience the same event as happening at different speeds.

Irreversibility

Time only flows in one direction, from the past to the present to the future. This is why we can remember the past but not the future.

Sequentiality

Events in time happen one after the other. This is why we can say that one event happened before or after another event.

Duration

Events in time take a certain amount of time to happen. This is why we can say that one event lasted longer or shorter than another event.
